About Us

Kandy Software Inc., is established in Calgary, Canada.

What We Do

We are a software development and consulting firm specializing in custom software solutions for companies across the globe. Kandy Software Inc., offers a complete range of enterprise and distributed application servicesincluding eCommerce solutions, architecture planning, training, database design & development, strategic consulting, prototype development, creative graphic design and photo imagery, content management systems, content migration services, lead generation tools, advanced programming capabilitiesand SEO services.
